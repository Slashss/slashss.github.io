Time to Create!

- Open the VirtualAvatar Google Collab notebook by clicking the button

- Click on the "Runtime" dropdown menu in the top menu bar

- Click the "Run all" button in the pop-up window to confirm

- Now you just have to Wait for the algorithm to finish
